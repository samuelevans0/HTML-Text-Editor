Edit your website

No code, no prompting. Click any text to edit it, and drag photos right onto the page.

  1. Click Open my website and choose this website folder (the folder that holds index.html). Click Allow if asked.
  2. Hover text to highlight it, then click and edit. Use Ctrl/Cmd + B for bold, + I for italic.
  3. Drag a photo onto any photo spot (the hero, each service, Shawn's portrait) — or click the spot to choose a file.
  4. Click Save, then double-click publish.cmd to put it live.

Works best in Chrome or Edge. They save straight into your folder — photos included. (Other browsers fall back to downloading an updated file.)
